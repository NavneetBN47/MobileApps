About Contact Options and Warranty Status Information

By checking this box you are granting HP your permission to use your registration information, including the serial number and product number of your PC and any attached HP product (e.g. a printer or monitor), to provide you with personalised information about your products. Examples include:

  • Contact options to obtain help for your specific PC model (or attached HP product) including your country and language.
  • The warranty status of your PC to identify the HP support coverage for your PC or any attached HP product and provide you with warranty status notifications and extension options.

If you choose not to check this box, HP will not check your warranty status today. However, your HP product is configured to undertake warranty status checks and provide you with important status messaging at key stages in the lifecycle of your HP products. These warranty status checks confirm the status and coverage period of your HP product standard warranty and/or registered HP Care Pack service and report that status to you. Each message will provide you with information on how to opt out of future warranty status messaging for all HP products, or you can do so at any time by opening the Settings section in HP application (not recommended).

Why should I authorise this?

By selecting this option, HP will be able to communicate much more accurate information to you regarding support, contact options and warranty status.

Does this selection involve the use of personal information?

Yes. HP will use personal information provided during the registration process, including the serial number and product number of your PC (or PC component) or any attached HP product, in order to provide you with personalised information. No other personal information is collected or used without your permission. To read HP's Online Privacy Statement, please visit www.hp.com/go/privacy.

What if I change my mind?

The preferences you select here are not affected by the other contact preferences you select when you register with HP and can be changed at any time through HP application.
